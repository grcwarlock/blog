This is the first post on a new blog. The theme is built for long-form writing — clean typography, no distractions, and a layout that gets out of the way.

## How it works

Posts are written in Markdown and live in the `_posts` directory. Jekyll handles the rest: parsing front matter, generating pages, building the RSS feed.

The design leans on two typefaces. **IBM Plex Mono** handles headings, navigation, labels — anything structural. **IBM Plex Serif** handles body text, where readability matters most.

## What belongs here

Notes on things worth writing about. Analysis when something deserves more than a quick take. The occasional long read when the subject earns it.

> The best writing happens when you stop trying to sound like a writer and start trying to be clear.

That's the goal.
